Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

Design Blanks warehouse is located at 172 Pellisier Street Flesherton (Eugenia) ON. 

Do you have a retail storefront?

No, Design Blanks is online only with a contactless pick up option.

When will my order ship?

Our average processing time is 2-3 business days. This may be longer during very busy seasons. When your order ships you will receive an email notification. 

Need it super speedy? Add a Rush Fee and jump the queue. 

Can you rush my order?

Yes we can. Go ahead and add this RUSH FEE to your order and you will jump the queue and we will pack you and ship you on the next available truck.

Can I add something to an order that I already placed on the website?

Add to an order you have already placed and we will ship them together. Handy if you forgot something! Please follow the instruction in this ADD-ON link. It is a 2 step process. 

Cheaper than 2 full shipping fees. This combined shipping option can be used as many times as you like as long as you have an open order that you paid a full shipping fee on. 

**Be aware that in certain cases we won't be able to honour your add-on order. For example, if your order has already been packed it will be too late and we will contact you.

Can I cancel my order?

Cancelled orders are subject to a $12 cancellation fee. 

We are charged website and payment processing fees when you place your order. These fees are not refunded to us when you cancel your order. Therefore our cancellation fees are non-negotiable.

My order is missing something

Yikes! That is never good, let us know and we will do our best to correct this in a timely manner

Order Issues?  Contact us at admin@designblanks.ca  you must include 

- order number

- your name

- your email address 

- brief explanation of the issue

Include pictures, the first thing we will ask for is pictures of the problem and pictures of your packing slip. Mistakes happen and we are happy to help set things straight in a timely manner.

Do you offer bulk pricing?

We do not offer bulk pricing at this time.

I am ordering from the USA will I have to pay International taxes?

International taxes might be applied depending on your country's taxes policy. International taxes are your responsibility.

Customs duty is calculated as a percentage of the customs value of the goods:The percentage or rate varies depending on the type of goods. You can check the Import calculator for USA.

The customs value is made up of:the price paid for the goods,the insurance cost,the shipping cost.

What is your phone number?

We don't have a business phone number. Strange right? but that is often the way online businesses are run. 

We offer customer support by email at admin@designblanks.ca, through our contact form or send us a message through our Design Blanks Facebook page. 

Messages are answered between 10-5 Mon-Fri. We try to respond within 1-2 business days.

Shipping

Where do you ship to?

We ship to Canada and the continental USA.

What are my shipping options?

We offer shipping by Canada Post Expedited,  Purolator Ground, or Purolator Express. 

Couriers do not move mail on holidays, Saturdays, or Sundays. Delivery expectations are quoted in business days. Adverse weather, long weekends and holidays may also delay delivery.

PLEASE NOTE - The shipping option you chose does not alter our order processing time. It only determines your delivery service.

How long does shipping take?

Design Blanks ships to Canada and the continental USA. Orders are generally shipped within 24-72 hours (Mon-Fri) depending on the season. This may be longer during holiday and peak seasons. You will receive an email confirmation with your tracking when your order has shipped. Please allow up to 48hrs for your tracking number to update.

Orders are shipped by Canada Post Expedited,  Purolator Ground, or Purolator Express or you may send us a shipping label. Standard delivery is 2-7 business days.

Couriers do not move mail on holidays, Saturdays, or Sundays. Delivery expectations are quoted in business days. Adverse weather, long weekends and holidays may also delay delivery. Global pandemics cause uncertainty and require extra amounts of patience. Shipping delays are out of our control.

How much is shipping?

Shipping is based on the size and weight of your order. Shipping quotes will appear on the checkout page of the website. Shipping quotes come directly from Canada Post and Purolator. 

Orders over $160 qualify to ship for a flat rate to the following provinces - 

$15.50 ON 

$19.50  MB, SK, NB, PE, NS, QC.

$23.50 NL, BC, AB 

Flat Rate packages are shipped by the most economical option at our discretion. The flat rate shipping becomes an option at checkout once the minimum subtotal has been reached. 

Flat Rate Shipping is valid on orders shipped to Canadian addresses only.

How does it work if I want to pick up my order?

Place your order and select pick up when you are going through checkout. Your order will be ready in approx 2 business days. We will send an email to inform you it is ready and make arrangements with you at that time for pick up. 

Orders can be picked up from our convenient pick up bin outside the warehouse entrance.

What if I didn't select the right shipping option?

If you select the wrong shipping amount send us an email at admin@designblanks.ca If you selected ADD-ON order in error (without another open order in our system) 

The website will cancel and refund your order within 24-48hrs when it cant find the order it should add-on too. 

My parcel shipped but I haven't received it? or my tracking says delivered but I don't have it?

Check your tracking information. If the tracking information shows your parcel has not yet been delivered, or delivery has not been attempted, be patient and assured it is on its way.

**Expect a delay from when your label is printed until it gets picked up and scanned into the system. If the tracking shows it was delivered but you did not receive it, check your community mailbox, front door, garage, backdoors, porch. Sometimes your carrier will leave items in a safe spot. 

If you still haven't found it, please contact the courier with your tracking number and then contact us. Time is of the essence if a package has been delivered to the wrong address. We do not have any more information other than what is available on the tracking website. We can place a trace on the package through the courier. A parcel trace can take 10-14 days. 

Design Blanks is not responsible for lost or stolen packages. Risk of loss and title for items purchased from designblanks.ca pass to you upon our delivery to the carrier or, if such items must cross an international border, then risk of loss and title pass to you when they clear customs.

Return Policy

Can I cancel my order?

Cancelled orders are subject to a $12 cancellation fee. 

We are charged website and payment processing fees when you place your order. These fees are not refunded to us when you cancel your order. Therefore our cancellation fees are non-negotiable.

What if I have a problem with my order?

You are responsible for checking your order. Inaccuracies must be reported within 7 days from receipt of delivery.

Order Issues?  Contact us at admin@designblanks.ca  you must include 

- order number

- your name

- your email address 

- brief explanation of the issue

Include pictures, the first thing we will ask for is pictures of the problem and pictures of your packing slip. Mistakes happen and we are happy to help set things straight in a timely manner.

RETURNS - Returns are considered on a case by case basis and must be approved prior to items being returned. Return shipping will be at your expense.  

**** Vinyl is custom cut to order and not eligible for return or refund! ****

No refunds will be issued until return items are received by us. To be eligible for a return, your item must be 

- Unused/unopened and in the same condition that you received it

- It must be in the original packaging.

Under NO circumstances will washed and/or worn items or altered/customized items ever be considered for a return.

Our policy is 7 days from delivery. After 7 days we cannot consider any returns. We will only accept returns for the following conditions: 

1. Items that were originally and substantially defective. NOT minor flaws or imperfections.

2. We sent the wrong item in error.

All returns should meet the following conditions:

You must contact us within 7 days of receiving the order. Refund or Store Credit will be processed after we receive the return.

~Please do not contact us about something you bought months or years ago (it happens!)

 ** Customers are responsible for all return shipping charges and a 10% restocking fee may apply (this does not apply to orders where wrong items were sent)

Damaged  items It is common to see differences in style or colour between batches or lots. Frustrating but not considered a defect. If we are shipping you blue cups we do our best to make sure they are all from the same lot but they may not be an exact match to the order you placed previously. Each batch or lot is custom made for us. Wholesale items made Overseas have a small percentage of defects or "less than perfect" items. We screen for major defects. Our product prices are based on a small percentage of defects and cannot be refunded. 

If you find a small hole or a missed stitches - We see this occasionally. You are crafty! You are a maker! YouTube is a great resource to learn to sew a couple of small stitches. It will empower you!  You don't have to know how to sew but learning to place a couple of simple stitches is a great skill to add to your crafting resume. We will NOT refund an item for needing to have a few stitches sewn into it. This applies to a small hole on the seam of a teddy bear or a small hole on a tree skirt. We understand that this is frustrating but this is the cost of ordering bulk items from an online warehouse that orders in bulk from overseas manufacturers. 

Third Party Returns - We will not accept items that you have sold to your customers. If your customer is unhappy with an item this is your responsibility as the seller.
